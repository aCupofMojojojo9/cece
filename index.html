CECE's Dream

At CECE's Dream, we believe that education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty and unlocking a brighter future. We are a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing tuition assistance to students in developing countries who face financial barriers to accessing quality education.

Our mission is to empower underserved youth by supporting their academic journey - offering not just funding, but also hope, encouragement, and a chance to dream beyond their circumstances. Through partnerships with local schools, communities, and donors around the world, CECE Dream helps students stay in school, pursue higher education, and build the foundation for a better life.

Every child deserves the opportunity to learn. At CECE Dream, we're making that dream a reality - one student at a time.

What We Do

Empowering communities through education and creating lasting change for children in Liberia

Our Approach

At Cece's Dream, we believe that sustainable change comes from working hand-in-hand with the communities we serve. Our approach is built on three core principles: partnership, sustainability, and impact. We don't just provide one-time assistance; we build lasting relationships and create programs that will continue to benefit children and communities for years to come.

Our founder, David Ziama, maintains direct connections with schools and community leaders in Liberia, ensuring that our resources reach those who need them most. Through regular communication and on-the-ground visits, we assess needs, measure impact, and adapt our programs to best serve each unique community.

Our Programs

Educational Resources

We provide schools with essential learning materials, books, and supplies to ensure students have the tools they need to succeed.

Community Partnerships

We work closely with local communities, school administrators, and education leaders to create sustainable, long-term solutions.

Direct Funding

We provide financial support to schools for infrastructure improvements, teacher salaries, and program development.

Capacity Building

We support teacher training and professional development to enhance the quality of education delivered to students.

Making a Real Difference

Every donation, every volunteer hour, and every partnership we build translates directly into improved educational opportunities for children in Liberia. We work with multiple schools, providing support that ranges from basic supplies to comprehensive infrastructure improvements.

Our upcoming initiatives include David Ziama's trip to Liberia in March 2026, where he will personally meet with school principals and administrators, assess current needs, and deliver funding to support ongoing educational programs. This hands-on approach ensures transparency and maximizes the impact of every dollar donated.

Our Focus Areas

Primary Education: We prioritize early childhood and primary education, recognizing that a strong foundation is essential for lifelong learning and success.

Teacher Support: Quality education requires quality teachers. We provide resources and support to help educators deliver the best possible learning experiences.

Infrastructure: Safe, well-equipped learning environments are crucial. We fund repairs, improvements, and essential infrastructure projects to create spaces where children can thrive.
